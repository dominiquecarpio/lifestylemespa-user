Correction Specialist

Where Others Stop,
Katarzyna Begins

Corrections are not just a service — they are a calling. For Katarzyna, restoring what was lost or misplaced is the purest expression of her artistry. She has dedicated years to mastering the rarest and most complex correction work in aesthetic medicine, giving clients a second chance at the results they deserved the first time.

Full Medical Potency — Never Diluted Every neurotoxin and filler used at Finesse is pharmaceutical-grade and prepared at full clinical strength. Over-diluted product is one of the leading causes of poor results and the corrections we see daily — we never cut corners on potency.
01

Improperly Placed
Botox

Correcting neurotoxin injected into the wrong muscle groups — causing unnatural tightness, uneven relaxation, or failure to address the intended area.

02

Unbalanced Lip
Enhancements

Dissolving and restoring asymmetric, overfilled, or unshapely lip filler — replacing "duck lip" results with natural proportion and defined structure.

03

Droopy Eyelids &
Brow Ptosis

Correcting Botox-induced eyelid drooping or fallen brows caused by migration into the levator muscle — one of the most delicate and common complications.

04

Filler Migration &
Malar Mounds

Removing filler that has shifted from its intended placement — including the telltale puffiness under the eyes and swelling along the cheek bones from migrated product.

05

Overfilled Cheeks
& Facial Distortion

Dissolving excess volumizer in cheeks, temples, and mid-face — restoring natural contour for clients who were left with a puffy, overly round, or distorted facial structure.

06

Spock Brow &
Brow Asymmetry

Correcting the uneven, arched, or "Spock" brow appearance that results from imprecise Botox placement — restoring natural, symmetrical brow shape and elevation.

07

Lumpy, Nodular
Filler

Removing or redistributing filler that has formed visible lumps, nodules, or irregularities under the skin — common after incorrect product choice or improper injection depth.

08

Under-Eye Tear
Trough Complications

Correcting swelling, bluish discoloration (Tyndall effect), or misplaced filler under the eyes — among the most technically demanding corrections in aesthetic medicine.

09

Frozen Expression
from Over-Treatment

Addressing the "frozen face" look caused by excessive Botox dosing — through strategic placement and expert timing to allow natural movement and expression to return.

10

Uneven Jawline
& Chin Asymmetry

Restoring facial symmetry in the lower face — correcting filler-induced jawline irregularities, asymmetric chin projections, and unnatural definition from poor technique.

11

Nasolabial Fold
Overfill

Dissolving excess filler in the smile lines that creates an unnatural pillow-like appearance — rebalancing volume for a softer, age-appropriate result.
